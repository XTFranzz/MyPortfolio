CitySmilesRepo

Dental Management System

A professional dental booking and patient management system featuring an interactive 3D teeth model for dental diagnosis and treatment planning.

Problem: Dental clinics needed a digital way to track patient dental records, manage appointments, and allow dentists to accurately document and prescribe treatments for specific teeth areas.
Solution: Developed a comprehensive booking system with an interactive 3D teeth model representing 32 adult teeth with 5 sectors per tooth. Dentists can click on specific tooth areas to document problems and prescribe targeted treatments.
Key Features: Patient booking management, interactive dental chart with 160 clickable tooth sectors, treatment history, prescription tracking, and patient data storage.
Tech Stack: JavaScript, PHP, Three.js (3D modeling), phpAdmin, interactive canvas rendering
Impact: Improved dental diagnosis accuracy, streamlined patient record management, and enhanced client-dentist communication during treatment planning.
